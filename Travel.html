My favorite cities I've traveled to

Stockholm, Sweden

Stockholm is the capital of Sweden and one of the most beautiful cities I've ever visited. The city is spread across 14 islands, and you can explore it by boat, bike or foot. Don't miss the Vasa Museum, the Royal Palace, and the Old Town.

Seville, Spain

Seville is a vibrant city in southern Spain, famous for its flamenco dancing, delicious food, and rich history. Highlights include the Real Alcázar, the Cathedral, and the Plaza de España. Don't forget to try the tapas!

Amsterdam, Netherlands

Amsterdam is a picturesque city with charming canals, colorful houses, and world-class museums. Visit the Van Gogh Museum, the Rijksmuseum, and the Anne Frank House, or just wander around the city and soak up the atmosphere.

Prague, Czech Republic

Prague is a fairy-tale city with stunning architecture, lively bars, and a rich cultural scene. Highlights include the Charles Bridge, the Prague Castle, and the Old Town Square. Don't forget to try some Czech beer and dumplings!
